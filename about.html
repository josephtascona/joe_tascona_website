About

If you’ve been the victim of on-the-job injustice, Joseph N. Tascona is there to help. An experienced employment and labour lawyer based in Barrie, he has a focus on settling severance agreements, wrongful dismissal cases and employment contracts. Joseph N. Tascona provides a number of legal services related to employment, including Canadian pension plans, disability benefits, health and safety prosecutions and labour relations board issues. Joseph N. Tascona has been helping workers in the Barrie and Lake Simcoe regions seek justice for more than 25 years. Call today if you require assistance with severance agreements, workplace compensation and wrongful termination cases. We're here to help.

Labour Law

Joseph represents employers with respect to percedings before the Labour Relations Board, in such matters as Union certification, and unfair labour practices.

Employment Law

Joseph is an experienced ligator with respect to wrongful dismissal, and human rights matters, including employment standards and occupational health and safety procedings.

Real Estate Law

Joseph provides legal advice with respect to agreements of purchase and sale, he also engages in Real Estate transactions, which includes purchases, sales and mortgages for residential and commercial properties.

Wills & Estates

Joseph is involved in the preparation of wills and powers of attorney, and probate.
